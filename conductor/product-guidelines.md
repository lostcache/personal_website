# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity

- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist & Clean. Focus on whitespace and typography.
- **Color Palette:**
  - **Background:** Dark Mode Support (High contrast dark background).
  - **Primary:** Neutral grays / white.
  - **Accent:** Green / Teal (for links, buttons, highlights).
- **Typography:**
  - **Headings:** Modern Sans-Serif (Clean, bold).
  - **Body:** Modern Sans-Serif (Legible, approachable).
  - **Code:** Monospace (Distinct for technical content).

## Voice & Tone

- **Conversational:** Write as if explaining to a peer. Avoid overly formal jargon unless necessary.
- **Technical:** Be precise and detailed. Show code snippets where relevant.
- **Approachable:** Use "I" and "You". Share personal insights and learnings.

## Design Principles

- **Content First:** Design should support the reading experience, not distract.
- **Performance:** Fast loading times, minimal scripts.
- **Accessibility:** High contrast, keyboard navigable, screen reader friendly.
