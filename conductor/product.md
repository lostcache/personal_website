# Product Guide

## Vision

To build a professional personal website for harshdevisha.com that serves as a portfolio showcase, a platform for technical writing, and a central hub for professional identity.

## Target Audience

- **Recruiters & Hiring Managers:** Evaluating skills and experience.
- **Technical Peers & Developers:** Reading blog posts and tutorials.
- **Potential Clients & Partners:** Seeking collaboration opportunities.

## Key Features

- **Portfolio Section:** Showcase projects with case studies.
- **Technical Blog:** Publish articles and tutorials (MDX).
- **Social Hub:** Centralized links to GitHub, LinkedIn, etc.
- **Dark Mode Support:** Theme toggle for better reading experience.
- **Contact Mechanism:** Integrated form or clear contact methods.

## Success Metrics

- Increased professional visibility.
- Engagement with blog content.
- Inbound inquiries for roles or projects.
